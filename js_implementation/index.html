Information

  • Numbers that are solid bold are given and numbers that are hollow are your guesses
  • The game will only alert you when you have a completed board; it will let you place numbers where they can't actually go
  • You can save your current game by clicking "save game", saving the string of numbers somehow (text file, word doc, etc). When you come back, you'll be able to paste that string into "load game"

How to Play

  1. Select a number from the selector below
  2. Select what row and column you'd like to place that digit at
